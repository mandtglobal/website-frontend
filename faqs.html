Customer Service Team
Can We Buy Our Own Materials?

Generally, we procure materials to ensure consistency in quality and project standards. However, if there are specific items you wish to supply, please reach out to our customer service team for guidance on what might be acceptable and compatible with our specifications.

Do you do full construction?

Yes, we offer full construction services, including design-build or turn-key construction. This means we assist with everything from creating initial plans to constructing the project, ensuring a smooth process from start to finish.

What Payment Methods Are Available?

Our payment process is typically broken into stages, aligning with key project milestones. This approach provides transparency and allows clients to track progress alongside their financial investment. Specific details will be discussed and agreed upon in the initial consultation.Payments can be made via banktransfer or cash.

Do you finish projects that have already started?

Yes, we can complete partially started projects, provided that detailed plans are available. If no plans exist, we will need to create one before proceeding, which requires a paid design process and an initial site visit for assessment and planning.

Do you do commercial projects?

Yes, M&T Global Construction Group undertakes commercial projects, offering comprehensive services for businesses and commercial properties.

Can I meet with the CEO only?

Due to the CEO's demanding schedule, they may not always be immediately available. However, our team is fully equipped to address your needs and will keep the CEO informed about key aspects of your project. If a specific matter requires the CEOâ€™s attention, we will work to facilitate a meeting or ensure that they contact you directly at their earliest convenience.

Why is there a fee for the initial site visit consultation?

The site visit consultation fee covers the time and expertise of our professionals as they assess your site. This ensures we understand the full scope of the project and can provide you with accurate recommendations tailored to your unique needs.

Why do I have to pay for the preparation of the Bill of Quantity (BoQ)?

The BoQ preparation involves detailed work by our quantity surveyors to calculate all materials, labor, and other costs for your project. This document is essential for transparent budgeting, and the fee covers the time and precision needed to prepare this comprehensive cost estimate.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on size, scope, and complexity. During our initial discussions, we will provide an estimated timeline, which may range from several weeks for smaller projects to several months for larger or more intricate builds.

What happens if there is a project delay caused by M&T?

In the rare event that M&T Global Construction Group causes a project delay, we will communicate with you promptly and work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible. We take responsibility for delays within our control and will adjust the timeline as needed to minimize impact on your plans.
