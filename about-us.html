Welcome to M&T Global

Who We Are

M&T Global Construction Group is a dynamic leader in the construction industry, with a focus on creating meaningful structures that inspire, serve, and endure. From residential builds to large-scale commercial developments, we are committed to delivering high-quality construction services that meet the unique needs of each client. With over two decades of experience across Africa and the United States, we strive to bring transformative, sustainable projects to life, contributing positively to the communities we serve.

We Hold These Dear

Our Values

  • Integrity: We believe in transparency, reliability, and accountability in every aspect of our work. Our word and our work go hand-in-hand, ensuring that clients, partners, and team members trust our commitment to excellence.

  • Innovation: We constantly seek new ways to push the boundaries of construction through the latest technologies and creative solutions. Our goal is to pioneer advancements that create value and set new standards in the industry.

  • Customer Service: Our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We listen closely, adapt to their unique needs, and ensure their vision is brought to life with unmatched professionalism and care. Customer satisfaction drives us to go above and beyond in each project we undertake.

History

M&T Global Construction Group, initially founded as Njie and Family Carpet Cleaning Services in 1998, has undergone a remarkable transformation over the years. Starting as a carpet cleaning service, the company eventually pivoted to home improvement. In 2009, M&T Home Improvement expanded into the construction sector, initially concentrating on small-scale repair and painting projects. Over time, the firm, under the leadership of Mr. Mohammed O. Njie, ventured into the residential contracting domain. Its emphasis on quality work and client satisfaction earned it a solid reputation and positioned it to enter into profitable partnerships, setting the stage for global expansion. The company's success in maintaining high-quality standards and exceeding customer expectations enabled it to attract local contractors and investors, paving the way for successful bids on larger projects.

In November 2018, to reflect its global ambition, the company rebranded itself as M&T Global Construction Group LTD and incorporated in The Gambia. This strategic move signifies the company's entry into the global market, with plans for further expansion into West Africa and eventually covering the entire African continent. M&T's dedication to delivering quality construction services and maintaining a global outlook has positioned it as a formidable player in the construction industry. The firm has established itself as a powerhouse in the construction industry, with the initial plan of developing an end-to-end solution oriented underway and expanding steadily.

Mission

Our mission is to help all those who walk through our doors—governments, the private sector, and individuals—in Africa and around the world realize their dreams of building world-class structures that meet their needs and can compete globally with the best in the construction industry.

Vision

Our vision is to shape the construction landscape across the African continent and the world. We aim to collaborate with clients in ways that are both innovative and transformative, exploring new possibilities in architecture and construction to redefine what is achievable.

Motivation

Our motivation arises from the need to provide relevant structures in an ever-changing world. As the demand grows for spaces that serve as both personal homes and business hubs, it is essential that structures be both aesthetically pleasing and functional. This is the value we strive to deliver.

Dedication

We are committed to delivering international-standard quality work to our clients. Our dedication ensures that our labor brings their dreams to life, often surpassing expectations. We are driven by a passion for innovation, creating solutions that meet both current and future needs.
